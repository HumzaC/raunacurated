Meet the Founder

The Heart Behind Raunak Curated

A look at Sameea Hussain’s passion for planning moments that feel like magic.

Hi, I’m Sameea Hussain.

I’ve been planning events for about five years now, and for each one I pour a bit of my soul into the process. I’ve always loved planning for the people I hold close — I think that’s my love language. I design events that work beautifully for families across every age and every stage.

If you choose me, I promise you won’t be disappointed. From listening to every idea to adding the unexpected details that make you say “WOW, I never would have thought of that!” — planning your event will be seamless, with an open line of communication from the very first conversation.
